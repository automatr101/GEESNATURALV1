Storage Instructions

  • Keep refrigerated at all times (2–8°C)
  • Consume within 5 days of opening
  • Shake well before serving
  • Best served chilled over ice
  • Do not freeze — this may affect the natural flavour

💡 Pro tip: Add a squeeze of fresh lime and a few mint leaves for an extra refreshing experience!
