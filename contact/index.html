FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the questions we hear most often.

How long do your juices last? +
Our juices are freshly pressed with no preservatives, so they last 5 days when refrigerated. We recommend consuming them as soon as possible for the best flavour and nutrition.
Do you deliver outside Accra? +
Currently, we deliver across Greater Accra. We're working on expanding to Kumasi and other regions soon. Contact us for special arrangements for other areas.
Are your juices suitable for diabetics? +
Our juices contain natural fruit sugars. We recommend consulting your doctor if you have diabetes. We can provide detailed nutritional information for each product to help you make an informed choice.
Do you offer wholesale or bulk orders? +
Yes! We offer special pricing for restaurants, hotels, offices, and events. Contact us via the form above or WhatsApp to discuss your requirements and get a custom quote.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept all major payment methods through Paystack, including mobile money (MTN, Vodafone, AirtelTigo), debit/credit cards, and bank transfers.
Can I return or exchange a product? +
Due to the perishable nature of our products, we cannot accept returns. However, if there's an issue with your order — damaged bottles, wrong items — please contact us within 24 hours and we'll make it right.
